Days of SAM

SAM has three days ment for three different subjects. Tuesday is the day where we cover software, for example HTML and students will learn how to make websites like this. Wenesday is the day where we teach Artificial Intelligence and will educated the children about what it is, and work on some projects that use Artificial Intelligence. The last day is Thursday in where we cover Mobile Development. This is a time where students can learn how to create their very own mobile app and publish it on the playstore
